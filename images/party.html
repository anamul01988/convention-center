    Best Tea Party Ideas

    By Sonja Masnikosa

    Are you tired of throwing parties in conventional and traditional manner? If the answer is positive, we've got something exciting for you. Why don't you throw a tea party instead? That is something that your friends and family probably don't expect to see so why not surprise them? It's fun, exciting and definitely different. That is why we are going to share some of the best tea party ideas that will make any tea party unforgettable. Here is what you need and how to do it. Enjoy.

    Tea Part Ideas - Pick a Unique Theme

    Any party is so much better if it has a unique theme. Whether you are thinking Queen Elizabeth, Prince Harry or any other royalty, make sure that your decorations and dress code match. If you want to really give your party the royal treatment, you can do that by encouraging your friends and family to wear dresses and hats in order to better represent British royalty. Or, you can tell your friends to dress up like one of Alice in Wonderland characters if you are not up for a royal theme.

    Tea Party Ideas - Arrival drinks

    This is one of the classics. Always make sure the sugar and milk is already on the table ready for the guests as they sit down. Providing a variety of tea - peppermint, Earl Grey, camomile, fruit, herbal, English Breakfast is the best thing you can do. Iced tea is also a great solution for a more refreshing drink in warmer weather. You could also serve up orange juice-based mimosa in order to have more choices to choose from.

    Scones & Scone Toppings

    Scones are definitely one of the most popular snacks for tea parties. They can be savory or sweet, and complex or plain. From basic buttermilk scones, sweet cinnamon scones up to savory cheddar mustard scones, there are plenty of scone recipes for any style of tea party. If you're planning a tea party menu specifically for girls, serving raspberry scones should be one of the best tea party ideas.

    Set Up a Brew-your-own Tea Station

    This is definitely one of the best tea party ideas as it will make things even more fun. If you're a fan of a more creative vibe, setting up a tea station will create wonders. You can display a wide variety of fresh herbs, petals, and sprigs in china or mason jars. It can come along with fancy spoons, and guests can select their favorites. Place a kettle of hot water and infusers nearby so your friends and family can infuse their picks by themselves.

    Bring Your Own Teacup Kind of Party

    You don’t have a formal tea service or simply lack fancy tea cups? Don't worry about it as you can make a theme out of it. Encourage your friends to bring a unique cup to use at the party. It can either be from their own cupboard or from a unique thrift store. This will make the party even more interesting as your guests will have their favorite cups while enjoying tea.

    Finish with Tea Cocktails

    Tea parties are no longer just a traditional British thing. You can always pull out the tequila and tonic water as that will take your tea party to the next level. It's really fun and eloquent to make cocktails that fit in with the cup and saucer theme. A simple peach bellini and prosecco will do the trick. Moreover, matcha mint juleps and chamomile honey bourbon cocktails will allow you to stir things up a bit as wellt! There's no better way to turn up tea time.

    So, these are some of the best tea party ideas that you can use next time you throw a tea party. They are fun, easy to accomplish and will entertain your guests. In the meantime, visit Teabloom for many more tea ideas, recipes and interesting articles.
